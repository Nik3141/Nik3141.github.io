Name That Piece: Classical Music Edition

This playlist has every classical music piece I know of (excluding the ones that I don't like). By 'classical', I mean instrumental and choral works in the Western style from about 1600 to present day, encompassing the Baroque, Classical, Romantic, and Contemporary periods. Right now, the playlist is pretty Eurocentric, so I've been trying to learn about 'classical' music from other cultures, so I can diversify it a bit. Some of the choral music from the Renaissance period is pretty epic too, so in the future that's another area I'd like to expand. I was inspired to create the playlist when I saw a 'Name that tune' playlist of pop music, but since I basically exclusively listened to classical at the time, I figured that I might as well make something similar but for classical.


Video Game Music

Like a lot of other people my age, I've played several video games which either really made an impression on me, had really good soundtracks, or both. I have a lot of really happy memories playing games like Little Big Planet, Sky Crawlers, and definitely Minecraft. The soundtracks for these games are sometimes good music by themselves, and other times they have a certain hit of nostalgia, but either way I sometimes like listening to them. That's pretty much why I made this playlist.
